#crontab #advance 

The smallest unit in cron is **minutes**. crontab doesn't support seconds. 

However there is a small trick which can help us to use seconds.
You schedule the same job **3 times** in one crontab entry, with `sleep` delays:

```bash
* * * * * /path/to/script.sh
* * * * * sleep 20 ; /path/to/script.sh
* * * * * sleep 40 ; /path/to/script.sh
```

This runs the script at 0s, 20s, and 40s of every minute — effectively every 20 seconds. You can adjust the sleep values, but you can't go below 1 second precision, and it's not perfectly reliable for critical tasks.

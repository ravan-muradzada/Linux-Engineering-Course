## **What is Stratis?**

**[Stratis](https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_enterprise_linux/8/html/managing_storage_devices/setting-up-stratis-file-systems)** is a local storage management solution for [[02. Introduction to Red Hat|RHEL]]. It is focused on simplicity, ease of use, and gives you access to advanced storage features. Stratis runs as a service to manage pools of physical storage devices, simplifying local storage management with ease of use while helping you set up and manage complex storage configurations.
